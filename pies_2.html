Cinnamon Apple Pie

Ingredients:

2 unbaked pie crusts (homemade or store-bought)
6 cups thinly sliced and peeled apples (use a mixture of sweet and tart apples)
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
1 egg, beaten
Coarse sugar for sprinkling on top (optional)


For the Cinnamon Streusel Topping:

1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
In a large bowl, mix together the sliced apples, sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt until well combined.
Place one pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and gently press it into the bottom and up the sides of the dish.
Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust, making sure to distribute the apples evenly.
Dot the top of the apples with small pieces of butter.
To make the cinnamon streusel topping, whisk together the flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a medium bowl. Add the melted butter and mix until the mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle the streusel over the top of the apples.
Place the second pie crust over the streusel and crimp the edges of the two crusts together.
Use a sharp knife to cut a few slits in the top of the pie crust to allow steam to escape.
Brush the top of the crust with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar (if using).
Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
Allow the pie to cool completely before slicing and serving.
Enjoy your delicious apple and cinnamon pie with a delicious streusel topping!
