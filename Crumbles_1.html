Plain Apple Crumble

Ingredients:

6 cups thinly sliced and peeled apples (use a mixture of sweet and tart apples)
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
In a large bowl, mix together the sliced apples, granulated sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice until well combined.
Pour the apple mixture into a 9-inch square baking dish.
In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, rolled oats, and brown sugar.
Pour the melted butter over the dry mixture and stir until it becomes a crumbly texture.
Sprinkle the crumble mixture over the top of the apple mixture.
Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
Allow the apple crumble to cool for a few minutes before serving.
Enjoy your delicious and simple apple crumble, which can be served warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream for a decadent dessert!
