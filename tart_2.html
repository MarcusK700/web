Apple and Blackberry Tart

Ingredients:

For the crust:

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
2-3 tablespoons ice water


For the filling:

4-5 medium-sized apples, peeled and thinly sliced
2 cups fresh or frozen blackberries
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt for the crust. Cut in the butter using a pastry cutter or fork, until the mixture resembles coarse sand. Add the ice water, and mix until the dough comes together.
Form the dough into a ball, and flatten it into a disk. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
In another mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, blackberries, sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, and nutmeg for the filling. Toss everything together until the fruit is evenly coated.
Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface into a circle about 1/4 inch thick. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom, and trim the excess dough from the edges.
Pour the apple and blackberry filling into the tart shell, spreading it evenly.
Bake the tart for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the fruit is bubbling.
Allow the tart to cool on a wire rack for at least 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
Enjoy your delicious and fruity apple and blackberry tart!
